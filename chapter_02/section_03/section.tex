\section{Alerts} \label{sec:}

Alerts are simple \emph{text boxes} that could provide the reader with more informations.
Below are the different types of \emph{alerts} used in this document.

\alertNote{Highlights information that the reader should take into account.}

\alertTip{Additional info and tips.}

\alertImportant{Provides crucial information.}

\alertWarning{Critical content that demands readers attention.}

\alertCaution{Critical content that could cause negative consequences.}
